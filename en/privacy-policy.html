Photo Coordinates Privacy Policy

This page outlines how Photo Coordinates handles analytics, cookies, affiliate links, and information that may be provided when contacting the site.

Last updated: 2026-04-04

1. About this site

Photo Coordinates is an independently operated website that explores the history of photography through photographers, movements, ideas, and historical context.

2. Google Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics. Google Analytics may use Cookies and similar technologies to collect information such as pages viewed, device type, time spent on the site, and referral sources.

This information is used to understand general usage patterns and improve the site. It is typically handled in a way that does not directly identify individual visitors.

3. Cookies

This site may use Cookies for analytics and basic site optimization. You can disable Cookies through your browser settings, although some features or measurements may not work as intended.

4. Amazon Associates and affiliate links

Photo Coordinates participates in the Amazon Associates Program. Some links to books and related materials may be affiliate links, which means the site operator may earn a referral fee if a purchase is made through those links.

As an Amazon Associate, Photo Coordinates earns from qualifying purchases.

Books and materials are selected based on their relevance to the history of photography and the understanding of photographers and movements featured on the site.

5. Information provided through contact

If you contact the site operator by email or other available means, information such as your name, email address, and message content may be collected in order to respond and handle the inquiry.

Such information will be used only for communication, support, and related follow-up.

6. Handling of personal information

Any personal information obtained through direct contact will be handled appropriately and will not be used for unrelated purposes or shared with third parties without a proper reason, except where required by law.

7. External services

This site includes links to and features from external services such as Google Analytics and Amazon. Please refer to the privacy policies of those services for information on how they handle data on their own platforms.

8. Policy updates

This policy may be updated as needed to reflect changes in site operations, tools, or legal requirements. Any revised version will become effective when posted on this page.

This policy may be revised as the use of Google Analytics, Amazon Associates, or site features changes over time.
