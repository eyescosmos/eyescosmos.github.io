What Is Photo-Secession?

Photo-Secession refers to a group founded by Alfred Stieglitz in New York in 1902.1

Where It Sits in the History of Photography

On this site, photographers connected to Photo-Secession appear mainly in 1890–1910s, often overlapping with Pictorialism and Straight Photography.2
