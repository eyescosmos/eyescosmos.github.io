Photojournalism

Photojournalism is an important thread within the history of photography. It can be understood as the professional practice of reporting current events through photography in collaboration with news media. This page follows the photographers, eras, and related contexts connected to it.

Overview

The professional practice of reporting current events through photography in collaboration with news media. The success of LIFE magazine from 1936 and the founding of Magnum Photos in 1947 helped secure photographers’ authorship and independence.
